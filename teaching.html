How to Embed a Google Map

In this tutorial, I will show you how to embed a map in your website. This will give your users a better view of a location than just an image, because they can zoom in and out and pan around.

To begin, go to Google Maps. This tutorial works with the older version of Google Maps, so if the map takes up the full browser window, you must temporarily change back.

Click on the button with a question mark in the lower right hand corner and select "Return to classic Google Maps". Find the area you would like to showcase and look for the link button beside the pan controls. It looks like a link from a chain.

You should see two text boxes, one with a link and another one with text that begins with <iframe> copy that code and paste it into your HTML file.

The iframe functions kind of like a browser window inside of a browser window, making it very useful for embedding content. A similar process can be used to embed Youtube videos, by looking under the share tab.
